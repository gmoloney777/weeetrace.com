Audit-ready traceability for WEEE recycling operations

WEEETrace is a mobile-first platform that provides verifiable chain-of-custody records for regulated WEEE streams — from intake to final processing.

Discuss a pilot

The problem

WEEE recycling involves multiple hand-offs, regulated processes, and increasing audit and reporting requirements. Many operators still rely on spreadsheets or systems not designed to provide defensible proof of compliant handling.

The solution

WEEETrace captures key operational events at source and converts them into clear, audit-ready compliance records — without disrupting existing workflows.

How it works

Operators record intake and processing events using a simple mobile interface. Each item or batch builds a time-stamped chain-of-custody record, which can be exported as audit-ready documentation when required.

Who it’s for

WEEE recycling facilities and contractors handling regulated electronic waste streams who need reliable, regulator-defensible traceability.

Why this approach

Built by a technical founder with over 15 years’ experience delivering production systems in regulated energy and recycling environments, with a focus on correctness, audit defensibility, and practical deployment.
