The problem

WEEE recycling involves multiple hand-offs, regulated processes, and increasing audit and reporting requirements. Many operators still rely on spreadsheets or systems not designed to provide defensible proof of compliant handling.

The solution

WEEETrace captures key operational events at source and converts them into clear, audit-ready compliance records — without disrupting existing workflows.

Audit-ready evidence

Generate defensible records for internal audits, regulators, and customers without manual collation.

Mobile-first capture

Simple on-site data capture designed for industrial conditions and variable connectivity.

Chain-of-custody history

Time-stamped event history per item or batch to support traceability and proof-of-processing.

How it works

Capture the key events that matter, store them securely, and generate the outputs auditors actually need.
